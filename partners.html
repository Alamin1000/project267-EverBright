SALES

Simple, smooth sales process from your very first hello to PTO.

Create a new lead in minutes with EverBright’s fully integrated address lookup tool auto-populates real-time data for utilities, incentives, weather, and typical energy usage.

Send your lead to our free Design Desk and get a complete proposal back in less than 30 minutes.

DESIGN

Customize all of your projects. Then, perfect them.

Create a custom solar system design in minutes and show the option to add panels, battery storage, and other energy-saving products.

Take your designs to the next level with HD imagery, advanced production validation, and automated shade analysis.

PROPOSALS

Don’t just sell them. Inform and inspire them.

Homeowners often expect to see energy savings from their investment, but it's seeing the right insights and making a connection that will sell them on the concept of going solar. That's where we help your business shine.

EverBright offers white-label proposal templates that give your customers everything they need to make an informed decision and allow you to integrate your brand into the proposal.

FINANCING

A suite of financing options to offer. A simple approval process.

After presenting financing options to your customers and providing a customized quote, you’ll be able to pre-qualify them in minutes by running a single soft credit score check, which won’t impact their credit.

Sending contracts and all other project specific details via e-signature also helps speed up the process.

OPERATIONS

Streamline projects end-to-end. Make the post-sale process a breeze.

Manage the entire post-sale process through project milestones and completed tasks. You’ll typically receive payments within 48 business hours of the milestone being approved.

Track payments, send out documents for approval, and stay up to date every step of the way by turning on notifications.
